About e-Sistemi d.o.o.

e-Sistemi d.o.o. is a Croatian IT company established in 2008, succeeding Intrasoft d.o.o., specializing in modern business software solutions and integration.

Our services consist of preparing companies for the digitalization of their business processes by introducing Luceed ERP or Odoo ERP/CRM software solutions, website development, creation of webshops integrated with Luceed/Odoo ERP systems, hosting solutions and business optimization in more mature business phases.
